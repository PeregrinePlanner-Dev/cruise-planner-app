Legal & Privacy

Effective: June 2026  ·  Path A pilot

What Peregrine Is

Peregrine is a cruise planning tool, not a travel agency. It is designed to help travelers organize their ideas and preferences so a travel advisor can assist them more effectively. Peregrine does not sell travel, quote live pricing, confirm availability, or make bookings of any kind.

What Information We Collect

When you use the planner, we collect the information you choose to share during your conversation — such as your name, email address, destination preferences, travel dates, party details, and budget range. This information is used solely to build your trip profile and facilitate your connection with a travel advisor.

We do not collect payment information. We do not run advertising. We do not sell your data to any third party.

How Your Information Is Used

The trip profile you build is forwarded to a travel advisor — with your explicit permission. You control when and whether your profile is shared. A partial session that is never submitted is not forwarded to anyone.

Your information may also be used to allow you to resume a planning session at a later time using a personal link sent to your email address.

Data Storage and Security

Your profile and conversation data are stored securely using industry-standard cloud infrastructure. Data is retained to allow session continuity and advisor follow-up. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

No Guarantees

Cruise recommendations, destination suggestions, and itinerary information provided through Peregrine are for planning purposes only. Availability, pricing, and itinerary details change frequently and must be confirmed with your travel advisor before any booking decision is made. Peregrine makes no representation that any specific cruise, ship, or itinerary is available or accurately priced.

No Obligation

Use of this planner does not create any obligation to book travel, engage a travel advisor, or share your information with any party. You may exit the planner at any time. Only information you explicitly submit is forwarded.

Terms of Use

By using Peregrine, you agree that the platform is provided as a planning aid only. You agree not to rely on any information provided through the planner as a confirmed booking, a price guarantee, or a representation of current availability. All actionable decisions — booking, pricing, itinerary confirmation — must be made in consultation with a travel advisor.

Peregrine reserves the right to update these terms at any time. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of any updated terms.
